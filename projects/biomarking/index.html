The EXtractor Architecture#

  • Backbone: (Standard CNN Classifier)

The Critic Architecture#

  • Framework: (Wasserstein GAN with Gradient Penalty (WGAN-GP))
  • Purpose: (Enforcing the 1-Lipschitz constraint to prevent mode collapse and ensure visual realism)

The Biometric Matcher#

  • State: (Frozen weights (Non-trainable))
  • Mechanism: (Calculating Cosine Similarity loss betweenj Original and Watermarked identity vectors)
  • Gradient FLow: (How errors backpropagate through the frozen matcher to correct the Generator)
